Meet Tom

Who I am, why I’m building this, and the credo that holds it together.

I’m Tom Hafner. I’m interested in how complex systems regulate — and how they fail. Flip The Discourse is an attempt to examine attention, metacognition, and dialogue with both scientific discipline and lived honesty.

For many years, my personal system was far from regulated. That dysregulation affected not only myself, but those around me.

There were many factors that contributed to my sobriety, but each required metacognition — the ability to think about my own thinking – “stinkin’ thinkin’.”

I am still learning, and I intend to continue learning through this project.

My hope is that this work becomes a thoughtful and enduring resource — not only for myself, but for others navigating similar terrain. We are living in a moment where public discourse is often polemic. This project explores whether we can Flip That Discourse toward something more civil.

The Credo of Flip the Discourse

A short set of commitments for how I will write, listen, and revise.

If you believe I have failed to uphold these commitments, I invite you to let me know.

Email Me
  • I do not claim certainty.
  • I claim responsibility.
  • I will examine claims before I amplify them.
  • I will seek primary sources before conclusions.
  • I will steelman positions before critiquing them.
  • I believe tension arises when information channels close.
  • I believe disciplined dialogue can reopen them.
  • I will update when evidence changes my mind.
  • I will admit error when I am wrong.
  • I will not dehumanize those with whom I disagree.
  • The goal is not victory.
  • The goal is clarity.
